# Email Marketing Campaign Analysis (SQL)

## Objective
Analyze email marketing data to measure campaign effectiveness,
customer behavior, and revenue impact using SQL.

## Dataset
- 5,000 rows of email marketing data
- Campaigns, customers, devices, cities, revenue

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- Excel

## Key Insights
-The Festival Offer campaign generated the highest number of purchases among all campaigns.
-Out of 5,000 emails sent, 972 customers clicked on the emails, and 280 customers completed a purchase, indicating a clear drop-off at each stage of the engagement funnel.
- A small number of campaigns contribute most revenue
-Desktop and Tablet users show slightly higher email engagement compared to Mobile users, with higher open and click-through rates.
